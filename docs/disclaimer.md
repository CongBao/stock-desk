# Disclaimer

## Research use only

Stock Desk is software for local research and reproducible experimentation. It
does not provide investment, legal, tax, accounting, or personalized financial
advice. It does not connect to brokers, place orders, manage a portfolio, or
promise any return. Nothing in the interface, reports, examples, documentation,
or generated output is a recommendation to buy, sell, or hold a security.

## Data limitations

Market, fundamental, announcement, and news data can be delayed, incomplete,
incorrect, revised, adjusted, permission-limited, or unavailable. Symbols,
calendars, corporate actions, price limits, suspensions, and provider coverage can
change. Provenance and cached snapshots improve auditability but do not prove
accuracy or fitness for a purpose.

Users are responsible for provider contracts, licenses, quotas, redistribution
rights, and applicable law. Do not assume that access through a provider permits
publication or redistribution.

## Model limitations

Model-generated analysis can hallucinate, omit evidence, misread context, or
produce internally inconsistent conclusions. Source links and rating suppression
reduce some failure modes but do not make output factual. Prompt injection or
malicious external text may still influence generated content despite bounded
inputs and role instructions.

Backtests are historical simulations, not forecasts. Results depend on data,
formula semantics, date range, costs, execution assumptions, and survivorship or
selection effects. Pool statistics are independent per-symbol trade samples and
never represent a shared-capital portfolio return.

## User responsibility

Independently verify source data, formula behavior, execution assumptions,
calculations, citations, and generated conclusions. Use qualified professional
advice where appropriate. You remain responsible for every decision and for
protecting credentials, local data, backups, and model output.
